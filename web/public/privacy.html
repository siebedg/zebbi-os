Privacy Policy

Zebbi OS · Last updated: 17 July 2026

Zebbi OS (zebbi-os.vercel.app) is a personal daily-log dashboard. It is not a public product for other users.

Whoop data

If you connect a WHOOP account, Zebbi OS requests read-only access to sleep data (wake time, bedtime, and sleep performance score) via the official WHOOP OAuth API.

  • Data is used only to fill your own sleep fields in Zebbi OS.
  • Data is stored in your private cloud database (Upstash Redis) tied to this app.
  • We do not sell, share, or advertise with WHOOP data.
  • You can disconnect WHOOP at any time in the app; OAuth tokens are then deleted.

Other data

Daily logs, reading progress, and weight entries you enter yourself are stored in the same private database and are protected by a PIN you set.
